Salad

Description

Salad of tomato, avocado pear and lettuce.

Ingredients

  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1 avocado pear
  • 8 lettuce leaves
  • 1 onion
  • 1 coup of cook oil
  • 1/2 spoon of salt

You also can add other vegetables, and dressing ingredients.

Steps

  1. Wash the aliments
  2. Slice the tomatoes
  3. Dice in small pices the avocado pear
  4. Cut into smaller portions the lettuce leaves
  5. Slice in litte parts the onion
  6. Put the vegetables together in a bowl and the rest of the ingridients
  7. Mix it all together

Enjoy your salad!
